# Acme SaaS Platform – Frequently Asked Questions

## Account & Authentication

### How do I reset my password?
To reset your password, navigate to the login page and click "Forgot password?". 
Enter your registered email address and you will receive a reset link within 5 minutes. 
The link expires after 24 hours. If you don't receive the email, check your spam folder 
or contact support@acme.com.

### How do I enable two-factor authentication (2FA)?
Go to Settings → Security → Two-Factor Authentication. 
Click "Enable 2FA" and scan the QR code with an authenticator app such as Google Authenticator or Authy. 
Enter the 6-digit code to confirm setup. After enabling, you will be prompted for a 2FA code at every login.

### How do I change my email address?
Navigate to Settings → Profile → Email Address. 
Enter your new email address and click "Update". A verification email will be sent to the new address. 
You must click the verification link before the change takes effect.

### Can I have multiple accounts with the same email?
No. Each email address can only be associated with one Acme account. 
If you need separate workspaces, use our Teams feature to invite collaborators.

## Billing & Subscriptions

### What payment methods do you accept?
We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover), 
PayPal, and bank transfers for annual Enterprise plans. 
Cryptocurrency payments are not currently supported.

### How do I cancel my subscription?
To cancel, go to Settings → Billing → Subscription and click "Cancel Subscription". 
Your access continues until the end of the current billing period. 
We do not offer prorated refunds for mid-cycle cancellations.
You can also email billing@acme.com for assisted cancellation.

### What is your refund policy?
We offer a full refund within 14 days of initial purchase for new customers. 
Renewal charges are non-refundable unless there was a billing error on our part. 
To request a refund, contact billing@acme.com with your invoice number.

### How do I download my invoices?
Go to Settings → Billing → Invoices. All past invoices are listed and can be downloaded 
as PDF files. Invoices are generated on the 1st of each month for monthly plans.

### How do I upgrade my plan?
Navigate to Settings → Billing → Plans. 
Select the plan you wish to upgrade to and confirm payment. 
Upgrades take effect immediately and you will be charged a prorated amount 
for the remainder of the billing cycle.

## Features & Usage

### What is the maximum file upload size?
- Starter Plan: 25 MB per file, 5 GB total storage
- Pro Plan: 100 MB per file, 50 GB total storage
- Enterprise Plan: 500 MB per file, unlimited storage

### What file formats are supported?
We support PDF, DOCX, XLSX, PPTX, PNG, JPG, GIF, MP4, MP3, ZIP, and CSV files. 
HTML and Markdown files are supported for text documents.

### How do I export my data?
Go to Settings → Data → Export. 
You can export all your data as a ZIP archive containing JSON files. 
Large exports are processed asynchronously and you will receive an email 
with a download link when ready (usually within 30 minutes).

### How do I add a team member?
Navigate to Settings → Team → Members and click "Invite Member". 
Enter their email address and select a role (Admin, Editor, or Viewer). 
They will receive an invitation email valid for 7 days.

### How many team members can I add?
- Starter Plan: 1 user (owner only)
- Pro Plan: up to 10 users
- Enterprise Plan: unlimited users

## Integrations & API

### How do I generate an API key?
Go to Settings → Developer → API Keys and click "Generate New Key". 
Give the key a descriptive name and set its expiration date. 
Store the key securely — it will only be shown once.

### What are the API rate limits?
- Starter Plan: 100 requests per minute
- Pro Plan: 1,000 requests per minute
- Enterprise Plan: 10,000 requests per minute (custom limits available)
All plans have a burst allowance of 2x the rate limit for up to 10 seconds.

### How do I integrate with Slack?
Go to Settings → Integrations → Slack and click "Connect". 
Authorize the Acme app in your Slack workspace. 
You can then receive notifications and run slash commands directly from Slack.

### Does the API support webhooks?
Yes. Go to Settings → Developer → Webhooks to configure webhook endpoints. 
We send events for: record.created, record.updated, record.deleted, 
billing.payment_succeeded, and billing.payment_failed.

## Security & Compliance

### How do I set up SSO (Single Sign-On)?
SSO is available on Enterprise plans. 
Contact your account manager or email enterprise@acme.com to get started. 
We support SAML 2.0 and OIDC protocols, compatible with Okta, Azure AD, and Google Workspace.

### Is my data encrypted?
Yes. All data is encrypted at rest using AES-256 and in transit using TLS 1.3. 
Encryption keys are managed by AWS KMS with automatic rotation every 90 days.

### Where is my data stored?
Data is stored in AWS data centers in the US (us-east-1) by default. 
Enterprise customers can request EU (eu-west-1) or APAC (ap-southeast-1) data residency.

### Are you GDPR compliant?
Yes. Acme is fully GDPR compliant. 
You can submit data deletion requests via Settings → Privacy → Delete My Data, 
or by emailing privacy@acme.com. Requests are processed within 30 days.

## Troubleshooting

### I'm getting a 500 Internal Server Error. What should I do?
A 500 error indicates a server-side issue. 
First, check our status page at status.acme.com for any ongoing incidents. 
If no incident is reported, try refreshing the page or clearing your browser cache. 
If the error persists, contact support with the request ID shown in the error message.

### The app is loading very slowly. How can I fix this?
Try the following steps:
1. Clear your browser cache and cookies.
2. Disable browser extensions that might interfere.
3. Try a different browser or incognito mode.
4. Check your internet connection speed.
5. Check status.acme.com for any performance degradation notices.

### I cannot log in. My password is correct but login fails.
Ensure Caps Lock is off. If you use SSO, make sure you are logging in via the SSO button.
If 2FA is enabled, check that your authenticator app time is synced. 
If the problem persists, try resetting your password or contact support.

### My files are not syncing. What should I check?
1. Ensure you have a stable internet connection.
2. Check that you have not exceeded your storage quota.
3. Log out and log back in to refresh your session.
4. Check the desktop app version — update if behind by more than one version.
